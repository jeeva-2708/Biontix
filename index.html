ABOUT BIONTIX

Biontix '25, organized by the Department of Biotechnology at Karpaga Vinayaga College of Engineering and Technology, Chengalpattu, is set to take place on March 8, 2025. This national-level tech fest brings together innovative minds from across India to celebrate advancements in biotechnology and cutting-edge technology. The event features exciting competitions like hackathons and research challenges, alongside insightful workshops and seminars led by industry experts. With thought-provoking tech talks, project exhibitions, and ample networking opportunities, Biontix '25 promises to inspire creativity and collaboration, empowering participants to push the boundaries of scientific discovery and innovation.
