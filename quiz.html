QUIZ

VARUM... AANA VARADHU...!!!

MODE : OFFLINE VENUE: KVCET EVENT DATE: 19th March 2025 REPORTING TIME: 9 : 00 AM EVENT TIME: 9 : 30 AM

General Rules

  • Each team must consist of 2-3 members.
  • Only one attempt will be allowed.
  • All the teams should complete the test within the allotted time.
  • The quiz will be conducted offline at the designated venue.
  • Participants must carry their college ID cards for verification.

Quiz Structure

Round 1:

MCQ Round (Elimination Round) [Timing: 9:15AM to 10:00 AM] Format: Multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

  • Each team will answer X number of MCQs within a time limit.
  • Scoring: Correct answer = +X points, No negative marking.
  • Elimination: Top-scoring 2 teams move to the next round.
Round 2:

Buzzer Round (Final Round) [Timing: 10:15AM to 11:00AM] Format: Teams will answer questions using a buzzer system.

  • Scoring: First team to press the buzzer gets to answer.
  • Correct answer = +X points
  • Wrong answer = -X points (negative marking applies).
  • The team with the highest score wins the quiz.
Conduct and Fair Play
  • No use of electronic devices, notes, or external help.
  • Cheating or misconduct will lead to disqualification.
  • The quizmaster's decision is final.
Prizes and Certificates

Winners will receive prizes and certificates. Participation certificates for all registered teams.
