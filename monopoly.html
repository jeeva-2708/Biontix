Monopoly

Trade with your friends in a world of technical knowledge and win Exciting Prizes.

MODE : OFFLINE VENUE: Sri Sairam Engineering College EVENT DATE: 8th NOVEMBER 2024 REPORTING TIME: 9 : 00 AM EVENT TIME: 9 : 40 AM

ROUND 1

At the start of the event, each team will be given a crossword puzzle with questions covering programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python. The questions will also include topics from Object-Oriented Programming, Computer Networks, and Database Management Systems. The test will be conducted on paper, with each team consisting of up to 2 members and a time limit of 30 minutes to complete the puzzle.

ROUND 2

The shortlisted teams will advance to the second round, where they will compete in a Monopoly-style game. Teams can progress by correctly answering the questions assigned to them. The top 3 winners will be determined based on the cash and properties they accumulate during the game.

The winning teams will get existing cash prize and internship offer
